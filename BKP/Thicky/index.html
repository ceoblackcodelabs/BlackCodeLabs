Who Is Thicky Sandra

UNAPOLOGETIC & UNFILTERED

"I grew up in the spotlight, but I built my own light."

Sandra Mbuvi, popularly known online as Thicky Sandra, is a Kenyan socialite, businesswoman, and content creator — the youngest daughter of former Nairobi Governor Mike Mbuvi Sonko. Raised in a high-profile political household, she's been candid about the pressure of growing up under public scrutiny, eventually relocating to the UK in 2023 to chase more privacy and personal growth.

She's currently studying journalism, broadcasting, media, and podcasting at the University of Brighton, with dreams of one day commanding a mic the way her idol Jeff Koinange does. Off-camera, she's a entrepreneur, a body-positivity voice, and a self-described certified dog mom — at one point caring for up to 14 dogs at home.
