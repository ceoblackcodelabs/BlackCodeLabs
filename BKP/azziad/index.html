Meet the Man

Born to Entertain.
Built to Last.

Steve Thompson Magana, professionally known as Oga Obinna, is East Africa's most dynamic entertainer — a force who has redefined comedy and media across the continent. His unique blend of humor, authenticity, and cultural insight made him the go-to personality for premium entertainment.

From leading radio shows to creating viral YouTube content with millions of views, Obinna brings unmatched energy to every platform. His multicultural Kenyan-Nigerian background gives him a rare comedic range that resonates across demographics.
