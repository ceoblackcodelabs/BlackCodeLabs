About Us

Crafting Celebration
Artisans in Nairobi

S&S School of Technical Skills is Nairobi's leading institution for events décor and styling. Nestled in Kilimani along Elgeyo Marakwet Road, we transform passionate beginners into sought-after professional decorators.

Our hands-on curriculum covers everything from grand weddings and ruracio ceremonies to intimate baby showers and corporate events. Every class is guided by industry practitioners with real-world event experience.
