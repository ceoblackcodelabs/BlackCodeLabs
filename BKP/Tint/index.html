The 80% Rule

THE FILM IS ONLY 20%

The installation is 80%.

Most tint jobs don't fail because of the film. They fail because of rushed installs, dirty environments, and careless workmanship. That's how you end up with:

  • ✦ Dust trapped under the film
  • ✦ Bubbles after two weeks
  • ✦ Crooked edges & blade-scratched glass
  • ✦ Burnt door panels & missing items
  • ✦ The classic excuse: "It will settle."

Once it's done badly, you can't unsee it. That's not a film problem. That's an installation problem.
