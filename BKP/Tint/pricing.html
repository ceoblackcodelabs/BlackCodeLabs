Common Questions

PRICING FAQs

Neither is objectively better. Both are world-class brands with excellent ceramic series. LLumar IRX offers 86% heat rejection at KES 80K while 3M Crystalline offers 96% at KES 101K. The real question is the installation quality — which is why you're here. We install both to the same exacting standard.

Every film we install comes with manufacturer certification. We can show you the roll, the batch number, and provide a signed warranty card. We never use grey-market or counterfeit film — ever. Your install also comes with a Tint Studio receipt tying your vehicle to the film batch.

Kenya's NTSA guidelines generally require front windows to allow 70% or more light transmission. Rear windows have more flexibility. We'll advise you on the right shade for compliance and comfort. We don't install anything that will get you flagged at a roadblock without clearly explaining the risk.

We use only genuine, premium films. We work slowly and carefully in a controlled environment. We redo any window we're not proud of. And we back every install with our KAZI SAFI guarantee — 100% refund if you're not happy. That kind of work costs a bit more. And it's worth it.
