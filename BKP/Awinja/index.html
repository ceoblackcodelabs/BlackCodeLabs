The woman
behind Awinja.

Born September 8, 1987 in Nairobi and raised in Eastleigh, Jacky Vike found her calling through theatre after high school. Despite early rejections, the late Charles Bukeko cast her as "Awinja" — Kenya's most beloved TV house-help — and a star was born on Citizen TV's Papa Shirandula (2007–2020).

She went on to Netflix productions Click Click Bang and Inside Job, and Kenyan classics Nairobi Half Life and Disco Matanga. Of Maragoli-Luhya heritage, she is a proud mother, the third-born of four, and one of Kenya's most powerful voices in comedy.

Actress Comedian Netflix Talent Content Creator Brand Ambassador OGW 2023

🏅 Order of the Grand Warrior of Kenya (OGW), 2023 — Awarded by the Government of Kenya for her extraordinary contribution to arts, culture, and national identity.
