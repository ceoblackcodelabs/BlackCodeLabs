My Story

Born to fight with honour,
trained to stand with purpose.

My name is Kai Tanaka, and I started my karate journey at age six — not because I wanted to fight, but because I wanted to grow. Every early morning, every bruised knuckle, every moment of doubt has been a lesson that has shaped who I am today.

Training under Sensei Hiroshi at the Shotokan Dojo, I've learned that karate is not just a sport — it is a way of life. The discipline I build on the mat follows me into the classroom, into my friendships, and into every challenge I face.

My Voice

From the
Heart

"Karate taught me that the greatest battles are the ones you fight inside yourself. Every time I bow before stepping on the mat, I remind myself — this is not about winning trophies. It's about becoming someone worthy of wearing this belt."

When I grow up, I want to open a dojo in my community — a place where kids who feel unseen can find themselves through discipline and respect. Karate gave me confidence when I had none. I want to pass that on. I believe every child has a champion inside them. They just need someone to believe in them first, and a dojo to call home.

— Kai Tanaka, Age 13
