We Believe Every Couple Deserves Magic

Founded with a singular vision — that love deserves nothing less than extraordinary — Lumière Weddings has become Nairobi's most sought-after wedding studio. We don't plan events. We compose symphonies of emotion.

Our team of dedicated artisans brings together years of expertise in design, logistics, floristry, and human connection. We listen deeply, dream boldly, and deliver flawlessly.
