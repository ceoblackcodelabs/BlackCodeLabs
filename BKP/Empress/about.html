My Journey

A Life Lived in Service to Spirit

From an early age, I felt a profound connection to something beyond the visible world — a deep, quiet knowing that spirit was real, guidance was available, and every soul held within it the answers it was seeking.

"I didn't choose the spiritual path. The spiritual path chose me — and I said yes with my whole heart."

Over the years, that calling transformed into a sacred vocation. I studied tarot with devotion, immersed myself in the ancient wisdom traditions, developed my intuitive gifts, and discovered a profound ability to see clearly into the patterns, energies, and possibilities within a person's life.

Through tarot, intuition, spiritual wisdom, and compassionate guidance, I help individuals gain clarity, healing, direction, and empowerment in every stage of life. My sessions are not performances — they are sacred partnerships, co-created in the space between us, guided by Spirit, grounded in love.

Today, with over 240,000 souls in my TikTok community and thousands of private sessions completed, I continue to show up every single day with one mission: to help you remember who you are, and to walk confidently into who you are becoming.
