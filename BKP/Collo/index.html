Who Is Collo Blue

THE DANCER
THE ARTIST

"Every move tells a story. Every beat is a brush stroke."

Collo Blue is Nairobi's most dynamic performer — a dancer, recording artist, and social influencer who has built a massive digital presence while staying rooted in the streets that shaped him.

As the founder and creative director of The Cluster KE, he leads East Africa's most exciting dance collective, blending Afrobeats, hip-hop, bongo, and street styles into an electrifying signature sound and movement.
