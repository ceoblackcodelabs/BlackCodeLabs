Meet the Leader

Hon. T. J. Oyugi is a visionary, progressive, and people-driven leader aspiring to serve the residents of Central Sakwa Ward with integrity, dedication, and transformative leadership. Guided by the principles of unity, accountability, and service to the people, he is committed to building a stronger, more empowered, and economically vibrant community where every resident has equal opportunities to thrive.

Raised with strong values of hard work, humility, and responsibility, Hon. T. J. Oyugi remains deeply connected to the everyday challenges and aspirations of the people of Central Sakwa Ward. His leadership is rooted in listening, inclusivity, and action-oriented solutions aimed at uplifting families, empowering youth and women, and creating sustainable development across the ward.

Through his vision for Central Sakwa Ward, he seeks to champion transformative initiatives focused on youth empowerment, job creation, quality education, accessible healthcare, modern infrastructure, and economic growth. He is passionate about supporting small businesses, promoting innovation and digital opportunities, improving roads and public facilities, and ensuring clean water and better sanitation services reach every community.

Hon. T. J. Oyugi also strongly advocates for transparent leadership, fair representation, and active community participation in decision-making. He believes leadership should be accessible, accountable, and focused on delivering real impact to the people. His agenda prioritizes empowering farmers, supporting education bursaries, enhancing security, improving sports and talent development programs, and creating opportunities that enable young people to build successful futures within the ward.

With a clear vision and a heart for service, Hon. T. J. Oyugi continues to inspire hope and unity among the people of Central Sakwa Ward. His mission is to foster sustainable development, strengthen community welfare, and build a ward where progress, opportunity, and dignity are accessible to all.
