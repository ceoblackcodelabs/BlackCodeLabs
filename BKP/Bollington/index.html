Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions

Are Bollington's programs TVETA accredited?

Yes. Bollington Training Institute is a registered training institution with TVETA Accreditation Number TVETA/PRIVATE/TVC/0199/2025, covering all the programs listed on this site.

What are the entry requirements?

Most certificate courses require a minimum of a KCSE certificate or equivalent. Some short courses have no formal academic requirement. Speak to our admissions team for course-specific details.

Do you offer flexible class schedules?

Yes. We offer morning, afternoon, evening and weekend classes so you can study while working or managing other commitments.

What payment plans are available?

We offer affordable fee structures with flexible instalment plans. Contact our admissions office to discuss a payment plan that works for you.

Will I receive a certificate after completing my course?

Yes. All learners who successfully complete their program and assessments receive a recognised certificate of completion.

Can I apply for more than one course?

Yes, many students combine a technical course with a short business or entrepreneurship course. Speak to our team about combination packages.
