Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a property viewing?
+
You can request a viewing directly from any property listing page, call our office, or fill out the contact form above. Our agents typically confirm viewing slots within 24 hours.
Do you handle properties for diaspora clients?
+
Yes — we regularly support clients living abroad with video walkthroughs, remote document signing coordination, and trusted local legal partners to manage the full transaction.
What fees does Reality Boris Properties charge?
+
Our commission structure varies by transaction type and is always disclosed upfront before any agreement is signed. There are no hidden charges.
Can you help verify a title deed before I buy?
+
Absolutely. Every listing on our platform undergoes title verification, and we can also assist with independent verification for properties found elsewhere.
Do you offer property management for rentals?
+
Yes, our rentals team offers full property management services including tenant sourcing, rent collection, and maintenance coordination.
