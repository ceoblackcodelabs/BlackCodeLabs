Market Insights

Key trends shaping Nairobi's real estate investment landscape in 2026.

📊

Rental Yields Holding Steady

Apartment yields in Kilimani and Kileleshwa have remained between 7–9% over the past 18 months, outperforming many regional peers.

🏗

Infrastructure Driving Growth

New road and rail links along the Nairobi Expressway corridor continue to lift land values in previously underserved areas.

🌍

Rising Diaspora Investment

Kenyans abroad now account for a growing share of off-plan apartment purchases, drawn by favorable exchange rates and remote management services.
