Property Description

Set on half an acre of meticulously landscaped gardens in one of Karen's most sought-after lanes, The Acacia Villa offers a rare combination of privacy, space, and architectural elegance. The home features five generously sized bedrooms — including a master suite with a private balcony overlooking the garden — alongside four full bathrooms, a formal dining room, and a sunken living area framed by floor-to-ceiling windows.

The kitchen is fitted with imported cabinetry and granite countertops, opening onto a covered patio ideal for entertaining. Mature indigenous trees, including several decades-old acacias, provide natural shade across the compound. The property also includes a separate two-bedroom staff quarters, a double garage, and a borehole supplementing mains water.
