How We Started

A Decade of Building Trust

Reality Boris Properties was founded in 2016 by a small team of agents who believed Nairobi's real estate market deserved more transparency. What began as a single office in Westlands has grown into one of Kenya's most recognized luxury property brands, with a portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and investment-grade assets.

Today, our team of 50+ agents and advisors serves clients across Kenya and the diaspora, guided by the same principle we started with: every transaction should be honest, informed, and built to last.

🎯

Our Mission

To make exceptional real estate accessible and transparent for every client — whether buying a first home, growing an investment portfolio, or finding the perfect rental.

🔭

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted luxury real estate brand, recognized for integrity, market expertise, and the lasting relationships we build with every client.
