Rooted in Kenyan Soil,
Growing Your Future

Waingo Farm & Agrovet has been serving farmers, households & agribusinesses across Kenya with premium quality farm produce, livestock products & agrovet supplies since 2009.

We believe every Kenyan deserves access to quality agri-inputs and fresh farm products. Our certified team of agronomists and veterinarians are always on hand to give expert advice tailored to your farm's needs.
