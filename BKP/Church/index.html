About Our Church

A Family Built on Faith & Love

For over two decades, Grace & Glory Church has been a beacon of hope in our community — nurturing spiritual growth, fostering deep relationships, and serving those in need with love and compassion.

Our Ministry

Church Projects & Programs

Discover the many ways we are making a difference in our community and beyond through the grace of God.

Education

Community Bible School

Free weekly Bible study classes for all ages, deepening faith and biblical understanding throughout the community.

Outreach

Bread of Life Food Drive

Monthly food distribution serving over 300 families in need, embodying Christ's command to love our neighbours.

Children

Joyful Kids Ministry

Dynamic children's ministry programs designed to nurture faith, creativity, and character from an early age.

Worship

Praise & Worship Team

Our vibrant music ministry leading the congregation in heartfelt worship through songs of praise and adoration.

Infrastructure

New Sanctuary Project

Building a larger worship space to accommodate our growing congregation and serve the community better.

Health

Community Health Clinic

Free medical outreach bringing healthcare to underserved families in our community with compassion and care.
