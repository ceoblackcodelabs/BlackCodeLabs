About the Officer

Geoffrey Omatoke
Mosiria

Hon. Geoffrey Omatoke Mosiria — popularly known as Hon. Geoffrey Mosiria — is one of Nairobi County's most visible, energetic, and results-driven public servants. Currently serving as the Chief Officer for Citizen Engagement & Customer Service at the Nairobi City County Government, he has built a reputation for going far beyond his official mandate.

Holding a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of Nairobi and an MBA, Geoffrey brings both academic rigor and fierce street-level pragmatism to public service. He previously served as Chief Officer for Environment, where he spearheaded Nairobi's CBD cleanup — clearing hawker hotspots, tackling noise pollution, and enforcing environmental bylaws.

What sets Geoffrey apart is his humanity. From dedicating a portion of his personal salary to charity, to personally rescuing and rehabilitating street families, he embodies servant leadership in its truest form.

Core Initiatives

Leading with Action

From street-level rescue operations to county-wide environmental reform, Geoffrey's work spans six pillars that collectively serve every Nairobian.

01

Citizen Engagement

Bridging the gap between county government and residents through direct outreach, community forums, and rapid complaint resolution for Nairobians.

02

Street Family Rehabilitation

Personally rescuing, rehabilitating, and reintegrating street families — giving Nairobi's most vulnerable residents a new beginning and restored dignity.

03

Environmental Leadership

Hands-on clean-up of Nairobi's CBD — clearing hawkers from main streets, improving waste management, and enforcing environmental health standards.

04

Child Protection

A fierce advocate against child abuse and domestic violence — working to ensure every child in Nairobi is safe, protected, and able to reach their potential.

05

Family Reunification

Actively working to reunite lost or separated individuals with their families — a deeply personal mission reflecting his commitment to community wellbeing.

06

Charitable Giving

Dedicating a personal portion of his salary every month to fund community service, social interventions, and support for vulnerable Nairobians.

Transforming Lives

From the Streets
to a New Life

Hon. Geoffrey Mosiria personally leads rescue operations for individuals living on Nairobi's streets. He doesn't just clear the streets — he invests in the people who once called them home.

01

Outreach & Rescue

Direct field visits to identify vulnerable individuals and street families, establishing trust and assessing immediate needs.

02

Rehabilitation & Care

Access to shelter, medical attention, counseling, and basic needs as the first step to rebuilding a stable life.

03

Skills & Reintegration

Connecting individuals with skills training, employment opportunities, and community support systems for long-term change.

04

Family Reunification

Tracing and reconnecting individuals with their families — restoring relationships and providing a foundation for renewed purpose.
