Who We Are

About Manyatta Boys High School

Manyatta Boys High School is a distinguished government-sponsored national school nestled in the heart of Migori County, Kenya. Since its founding, the school has stood as a beacon of academic excellence and moral uprightness in the Nyanza region.

Our institution is dedicated to the holistic development of young men — intellectually, physically, spiritually, and socially. With a rich tradition of producing Kenya's finest leaders, engineers, doctors, and professionals, we take pride in our legacy and the bright futures we help shape.

Guided by our motto "Knowledge, Discipline, Service", Manyatta Boys stands firm in the pursuit of greatness for every student who walks through our gates.
