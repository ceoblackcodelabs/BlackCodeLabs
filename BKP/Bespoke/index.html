Our Story

From The Street
To The Runway.

"I started as a hawker on the streets of Nairobi. Today I dress legends. Every suit carries that journey."

Austin Bolo — known to the world as Bolo Bespoke — built Bespoke City from nothing into one of East and Central Africa's most decorated tailoring houses. His philosophy blends modern luxury with Luo-inspired and African heritage, creating pieces that are unmistakably his.

From Prince Indah to Nadia Mukami, Bolo has dressed Kenya's politicians, musicians and culture icons — earning multiple fashion awards and a social following that spans millions across Instagram and TikTok.

How It Works

THE BESPOKE PROCESS

PHASE 01
Consultation

A one-on-one sit-down to understand the occasion, your style and your story. Fabric and silhouette direction begins here.

PHASE 02
Measurement

Precision body measurement across 30+ points. Nothing off-the-rack — every line is drafted to your frame alone.

PHASE 03
Fitting

First and second fittings to refine drape, structure and movement, with adjustments made by hand in-studio.

PHASE 04
Delivery

Final press, packaging and a personal hand-off at Bespoke City — or delivered wherever you are in the world.
