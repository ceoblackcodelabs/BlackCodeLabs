The Leader

Meet the Man
Behind the Vision

Hon. Mark Nyamita is a visionary, progressive, and people-driven leader aspiring to serve the residents of Uriri Constituency with integrity, dedication, and transformative leadership. Guided by the principles of unity, accountability, and service, he is committed to building a stronger, empowered, and economically vibrant community.

Raised with strong values of hard work, humility, and responsibility, he remains deeply connected to the everyday challenges and aspirations of the people. His leadership is rooted in listening, inclusivity, and action-oriented solutions aimed at uplifting families, empowering youth and women, and creating sustainable development across the ward.

Transformative Agenda

What We're Building
Together

A clear, actionable agenda for every family in Uriri Constituency — from the classroom to the clinic, from the farm to the future.

01
🎓
Education Transformation
Modern classrooms, digital labs, improved school transition rates, education bursaries, and teacher support programs across the ward.
02
🏥
Better Healthcare
Upgraded health facilities, improved medical services, expanded mobile healthcare programs, and reduced patient travel distances.
03
💼
Youth & Women Empowerment
Grants, business support programs, startup capital, professional training, and empowerment opportunities for local groups.
04
💧
Clean Water Access
Expansion of boreholes, solar-powered water pumps, and community water projects to improve access to safe, clean water for all.
05
🛣️
Road Infrastructure
Commissioning rural tarmac roads to reduce travel time, improve market access, and boost economic opportunities for farmers and traders.
06
⚡
Green Energy Initiative
Solar electrification of villages, schools, and health centers — bringing light, productivity, and prosperity to every household.
