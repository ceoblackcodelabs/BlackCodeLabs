The Duke of Kakamega

Entertainer.
Preacher.
Born January 17, 1991 — Nairobi

Dr. Ofweneke (real name Sande Bush Oyolo) is a powerhouse Kenyan entertainer — comedian, TV/radio host, MC, actor, author, motivational speaker, and leadership coach all in one extraordinary person.

Born with Luhya/Kamba heritage and raised in Nairobi, he channelled raw talent into one of East Africa's most diverse and celebrated media careers. Known for his signature Nigerian-style accent, catchphrases like "Oga oh!", and a gift for blending sharp comedy with deep wisdom.

A vocal Christian apostle, CEO of 92 Degrees Creative Hub, and founder of Creatives For Christ — Dr. Ofweneke is as comfortable dropping marriage wisdom ("Ndoa ni kisima kirefu…") as he is commanding a festival crowd of thousands.
