Meet The Cluster

The Cluster KE is an elite dance collective based in Nairobi, Kenya. Born from the streets and raised on stages, we blend African rhythm with global choreography.

From viral music video features to sold-out arena shows, our squad delivers unmatched energy and professional precision. Nyota na Manyota — we are the stars that make stars shine brighter.
