Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I subscribe?
After subscribing, you'll be assigned a project manager within 24 hours who will schedule a kickoff call to understand your requirements in detail. We begin development within 48 hours of the kickoff call.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and changes take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties or fees apply.
Do I own the code and assets?
Yes — 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, assets, and intellectual property transfer entirely to you. We sign IP transfer agreements for all projects.
What technologies do you work with?
We work across the full stack: React, Next.js, Vue, Node.js, Python, Django, Go, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin, AWS, GCP, Azure, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more. We pick the right tool for your project.
Is the Custom plan really unlimited?
Custom plans are scoped per contract. "Unlimited" refers to our commitment to deliver everything agreed upon with no artificial project limits. We define scope together during the discovery phase.
