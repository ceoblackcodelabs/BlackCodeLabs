Who Is The OG

THE ORIGINAL
GANGSTER

"Nairobi raised me. Hip-hop made me. The streets crowned me."

Born Brian Robert Ouko in Kayole, Nairobi, Khaligraph Jones is East Africa's most decorated rapper. With a career spanning over a decade, he has taken Kenyan hip-hop from the streets to global stages — BET Hip Hop Awards, collaborations with international legends, and a fanbase that spans every continent.

His pen is sharp, his delivery is surgical, and his stage presence is unmatched. When Khaligraph steps up, everyone else steps back. Wanajua ni mazishi.
