Meet the Media Messiah

Born to Command
Every Room

Azeezah Wanjiru Hashim is Kenya's most electric multi-hyphenate media personality — TV host, radio queen, international MC, DJ, voice-over artist, and influencer, all in one.

Born and raised in Nairobi's Eastleigh — from a bold mix of Bajuni Swahili and Kikuyu heritage — she balanced academic excellence at Kenya High School with an unshakeable passion for media. She followed that fire to the University of Nairobi, earning her BA and launching one of East Africa's most meteoric careers.

Today she hosts 10 over 10 on Citizen TV (Friday nights) and The Breakout on Hot 96 FM (10AM–1PM weekdays), with dreams pointed squarely at the global stage: BET Awards, E! News, and beyond.
