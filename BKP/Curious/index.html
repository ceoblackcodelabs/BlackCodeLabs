About Us

We Are The
Curious Cousins

Best Dance Nairobi Afro Fusion TikTok Trends

Curious Cousins is Nairobi's premier Best dance collective, born from the raw, pulsating energy of Kenya's urban dance scene. We don't just follow trends — we set them.

Our motto "Best Dancers" — Stars and Constellations — captures our spirit perfectly. Every member is a star in their own right, but together we shine like a constellation that lights up the night sky.

From viral TikTok challenges to live stage performances, Curious Cousins brings infectious energy, precision choreography, and pure African Best culture to every stage we touch.
