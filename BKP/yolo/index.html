Who We Are

Scooping Joy Since 2019

YOLO Ice Cream started in a tiny kitchen in Westlands with one churn, big dreams, and a very long spoon. Today we craft over 30 signature flavours using locally sourced Kenyan fruits, Belgian chocolate, and cream so fresh it still smells like the farm.

Every scoop is a tiny adventure. We don't just make ice cream — we make moments worth remembering.
