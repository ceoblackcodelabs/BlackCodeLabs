Meet the Leader

Hon. Walter Owino is a distinguished lawyer, accomplished policy expert, and passionate servant leader currently serving his second term as Member of Parliament for Awendo Constituency.

Born and raised in Migori County, Hon. Owino’s journey from a humble background to the National Assembly is a testament to his dedication, intellect, and deep commitment to public service. A trained advocate with years of experience in legal practice and governance, he brings a unique blend of legal expertise, strategic thinking, and grassroots understanding to leadership.

Since his election, he has spearheaded the Awendo Economic Revival Plan, championed key legislative reforms, and maintained an open-door policy that has redefined accessible leadership. His focus remains on inclusive growth, quality infrastructure, improved healthcare, education, and economic empowerment — particularly for youth, women, and smallholder farmers.
