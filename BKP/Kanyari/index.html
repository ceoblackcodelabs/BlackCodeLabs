15+ Years of Ministry

A Shepherd with a Father's Heart

Prophet Kanyari is a highly anointed servant of God, powerful preacher, and visionary leader who has dedicated over 15 years to transforming communities through the power of the Gospel.

Born and raised in Kenya, his calling was evident from a young age. Through his ministry, thousands have encountered Jesus, families have been restored, and countless young people have discovered their divine purpose.

"My mandate is simple — preach the Word, love the people, and trust God for the harvest."

His ministry spans crusades, discipleship programs, media outreach, and community development — a holistic expression of Kingdom values in East Africa and beyond.
